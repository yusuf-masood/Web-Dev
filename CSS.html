About KBTU

Kazakh British Technical University (KBTU) is a leading technical university in Kazakhstan, providing students with a high-quality education in engineering, technology, and business. KBTU offers a range of programs at both the undergraduate and graduate levels, taught by experienced and knowledgeable faculty.

Founded in 2005, KBTU has a rich history of providing students with a dynamic and innovative learning experience. With a focus on cutting-edge research and cutting-edge technology, KBTU is dedicated to preparing students for careers in the fields of engineering, technology, and business.

Location

KBTU is located in Almaty, Kazakhstan's largest city and the financial center of the country. With a rich cultural heritage and a thriving modern economy, Almaty is the perfect place to study and start your career.
